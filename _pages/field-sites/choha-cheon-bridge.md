## System Context

- Weekly field measurements were organized around simultaneous eight-channel strain sensing.
- The data acquisition workflow used QR-code-based access to support field-friendly monitoring records.
- The monitoring concept was intended to follow prefabricated bridge members across their lifecycle rather than only during a single inspection event.
