# Prefabricated Structure Transportation Monitoring System

Portable sensing work focused on monitoring precast concrete members during transportation-stage operations.

## Problem

Prefabricated members can be damaged during lifting, loading, transportation, and erection, but their structural response is rarely monitored continuously during these stages.

## My Role

- Built portable sensing workflows for precast member transportation monitoring.
- Collected and reviewed acceleration, tilt, and strain records during transportation stages.
- Supported gateway/cloud workflows for field-deployable monitoring.

## System / Methods

- Portable JANET sensing for acceleration and strain measurements.
- Gateway-based data collection for members transported by vehicle.
- GPS/cloud monitoring concepts for transportation-stage data.
- Response-history review for transportation safety.

## Field Deployment

- Applied sensors to precast panels and girders during lifting, loading, transportation, and erection.
- Supported field trials and iterative improvements to attachment procedures and gateway operation.

## Data / Outputs

- Acceleration, tilt, strain, and transportation-event records.
- Safety-related response histories during movement stages.
- Field lessons on sensor installation time, strap/support conditions, and transport-stage monitoring.
