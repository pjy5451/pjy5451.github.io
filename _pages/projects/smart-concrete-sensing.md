# Smart Concrete Sensing System

Smart concrete sensing work explores crack trajectory monitoring and long-term measurement-history visualization.

## Problem

Smart concrete and self-sensing materials require measurement systems that can capture resistance changes and translate them into interpretable structural or material behavior.

## My Role

- Designed sensing and data collection workflows for smart concrete measurement.
- Supported resistance-change monitoring and signal filtering for material response data.
- Built Python-based recording and visualization workflows for collected data.

## System / Methods

- Resistive sensing for smart concrete and crack trajectory monitoring.
- BLE/beacon-based data collection concepts.
- Median-filter-based noise reduction for resistance measurements.
- Long-term measurement-history visualization.

## Field Deployment

- Applied the sensing workflow to laboratory and prototype smart material monitoring conditions.
- Used the project as an early basis for later low-power and cloud-connected sensing workflows.

## Data / Outputs

- Electrical resistance histories and crack/sensor response records.
- Filtered measurement traces and visualization outputs.
- Prototype sensing concepts for smart material monitoring.

## Related Publications / Outputs

- Development of Low-Cost Wireless Sensing System for Smart Ultra-High Performance Concrete.
- Concrete Crack Trajectory Monitoring and Long-Term History Visualization.
