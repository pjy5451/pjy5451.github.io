# Freight Train Monitoring System

This project organizes field sensing workflows for freight and rail-related monitoring.

## Problem

Freight-car bearings are often evaluated through inspection-oriented maintenance, but vibration and temperature data can provide continuous evidence for condition monitoring during operation.

## My Role

- Organized field sensing workflows for freight-car bearing monitoring.
- Reviewed vibration, temperature, and operation data for freight safety applications.
- Supported cloud-dashboard concepts for repeated inspection and analysis.

## System / Methods

- Compact sensing nodes for bearing vibration and temperature monitoring.
- Base-station and cloud communication workflows.
- GPS-linked operation context for interpreting measurement changes.
- Dashboard-oriented visualization of bearing status.

## Field Deployment

- Applied the monitoring concept to freight-car field conditions with Koryeo Hwacha.
- Collected and reviewed field measurement logs for system stability and future diagnostic development.

## Data / Outputs

- Bearing vibration, temperature, GPS/location, and sensor-operation records.
- Dashboard concepts for bearing and track-related status review.
- Field data foundation for future condition-based monitoring algorithms.

## Related Publications / Outputs

- Development of a Low-Power IoT Sensor integrated with Cloud Services for Long-term Freight Train Monitoring.
- Freight-car bearing monitoring field records and dashboard prototype outputs.
