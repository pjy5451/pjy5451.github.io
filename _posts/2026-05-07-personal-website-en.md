Vibe coding has made maintaining a personal website feel much easier than before. Something I had only kept in the back of my mind finally became something I could actually start.

I am not sure where this site will end up. It might become another personal project that fades out after a few months, or it might become a useful URL that I can share when I apply for something. Either way, the reason for starting has not changed: I want to organize the work, experiences, and research traces I have built up so far.

So this is the first small record of getting the website back into shape.
